Hi, I am Joey.

I just graduated from the University of California, San Diego with a Cognitive Science - Human Computer Interaction degree this December. Design wasn't something I explored early on in university, I discovered it when I fell into a more than decade old path I created for myself because I thought I could be successful and happy by following a path that would not disappoint my parents. So I am excited to have found something I really enjoy doing and makes me excited to see my work develop from beginning to the end. I am always looking forward to learning and refining my skills in user experience design, interaction design, product development, and front end development. Currently, I am working at a language learning startup, where I design the user interfaces and optimize the user experience of the Android application.

In my free time, I am checking things off my bucket list (learning to surf), using Yelp as my tour guide for food adventures, teaching people how to swim, and trying to go on more backpacking trips.
