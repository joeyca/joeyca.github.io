AlchemyX

Mobile Application

Introduction

AlchemyX is a startup generator founded at UC San Diego that wants to bring innovation from campus. AlchemyX is looking to rebrand themselves with a website redeisgn and mobile application that aligns more closey with their mission.

The Challenge

In our needfinding process, we discovered one of the most important resources college students would want when creating a startup was resources. From this, we surveyed 100 college students about their trust in AlchemyX, the current services provided, and what other resources they would like to see provided. We found that there was a lack of trust in AlchemyX, there was no 'story,' and the resources listed on the current site were not specific enough. Our goal is have college students trust that AlchemyX will help them transform their ideas into startups.

My Role

I led the user research and the design of the mobile application, in addition to contributing to the design of the website redesign.

The Discovery

Affinitiy Diagram while brainstorming with CEO and CFO about their brand. Our mood board emcompasses what the mission the CEO and CFO has for AlchemyX. They wanted to make a positive impact by bringing ideas to life and teach college students how to create a startup. We drew colors from our mood board and felt the gray tones and green tones represented what AlchemyX wanted to portray.

Our stylescape for AlchemyX.

The Deliverables

Driven by our findings, we created a mobile application that tells AlchemyX's story and how it creates opportunities for both students wanting to join a startup and to create their own.

The Future

We are currently working on the final mobile application with the design team, CEO, and CFO and will be releasing it soon.
